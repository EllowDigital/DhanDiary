Privacy Policy — DhanDiary

Last updated: 12 December 2025

DhanDiary ("we", "our", "us") is a personal finance mobile application that helps users track income, expenses and savings. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal information (provided by you):
  • Name (optional — used for account identification)
  • Email address (used for registration, login, and backup/restore)
Transaction & financial data:
  • Income and expense entries (amount, category, date, note)
  • Transaction history and related metadata
Device & technical data (non-personal):
  • App usage statistics (anonymous)
  • Device model, OS version (for diagnostics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data only to provide and improve the App’s core functionality:

  • Store and display your income/expense records and history.
  • Provide analytics and charts about your spending.
  • Enable secure cloud backup & restore (NeonDB) when you opt-in.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve performance (anonymous).

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

3. Cloud Backup & NeonDB

If you enable cloud backup or sync, your data is transmitted to and stored on NeonDB servers in encrypted form. Backup is optional — if you never enable cloud sync, your data remains local to your device.

  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS, TLS 1.2+).
  • Stored data is encrypted at rest by NeonDB (provider controls encryption per their practices).
  • Only authenticated users can access their backups.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS) for all network communications.
  • Encrypted storage for backups and secure access control to backend credentials.
  • Regular code maintenance and security patches.

5. Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal or financial data with third-party marketers or sell your information. The only third-parties involved may include:

  • NeonDB — for optional cloud backup and restore functionality.
  • Authentication providers (only if you opt to sign in via Google or similar — used solely to verify identity).

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have control over your data:

  • Delete locally: You may clear app data or uninstall the app to remove local records.
  • Delete backups: Use the account settings to delete your cloud backup, or request deletion via email (contact details below).
  • Data portability: You may export your transaction history (if export feature is provided) and transfer it elsewhere.

7. Children

DhanDiary is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that such information has been provided, please contact us and we will remove it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will include an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
